The casino that wasn't: what happened on May 7

BlockDAG Exposed · May 7, 2026 · Based on 326,369 Telegram messages through May 7, 2026

May 7, 2026 was the date BlockDAG had promised for its casino launch. It was one of the most marketed deadlines in the project's history. As of 11:37 UTC on May 7, the official Telegram channel had made no announcement that the casino was live. The channel was instead posting "Casino Live Tomorrow" — the same automated message from May 6 — six separate times.

What was promised

The casino promise went through several versions in the week before May 7. On May 5, the official channel sent "Casino in 3 Days / Bets open in 7 Days" — already a split promise, with the casino opening and betting separated by four days. By May 6 at 22:01, the message had shifted again: "CASINO GOES LIVE — TOMORROW / Betting unlocks in 7 days."

Throughout May 7 morning, the official bot sent that same May 6 message six times at: 01:31, 02:31, 05:32, 06:03, 06:33 and 09:20 UTC. "Casino Live Tomorrow" — on the day it was supposed to be live. Not one of those messages said the casino had launched.

"Considering BlockDAG is the king of countdowns, should we not have a countdown for the Casino Launch? Considering how it was promoted as a life changer..." — Community member Zew MS, May 7, 09:04 UTC

What the community was asking

Throughout May 7 morning, community members posted variations of the same questions. "When does the casino launch?" "What time?" "Which website?" "Are there screenshots?" "Has anyone actually played?" Not one of these questions received an admin response on the specific topic of the casino being live.

The admin team did respond to other questions during this period. They confirmed staking was broken. They confirmed exchange deposits would open June 30. They directed users to the purchase portal. But on the core question — "is the casino live?" — there was silence.

The aftersale that closed forever

May 7 was also the date the aftersale was supposed to "close forever." The official channel sent dozens of "CLOSING FOREVER" messages throughout May 5 and 6. An admin explicitly stated: "No sale after May 7th."

At 11:09 UTC on May 7, official support was still directing users to purchase3.blockdag.network — the aftersale portal. No closure announcement was made in the analyzed period. Simultaneously, a new product called X10Swap was announced as "COMING SOON" — described as offering "up to 10X rewards" through "hourly activation cycles." The community immediately identified it as another sale mechanism.

May 7 promises vs reality (as of 11:37 UTC)

Batch 5 claims — no official date confirmed. Staking broken.

X10Swap — announced as "COMING SOON" with no date or mechanics

New confirmed date: June 30

One concrete new commitment did emerge during this period. On May 5, an admin confirmed: "Official deposits on June 30th." This is the first specific date given for exchange deposits — more than three months after the launch date of March 4. Presale vesting schedule coins were also confirmed to be inaccessible until after June 30.

June 30 is now the project's next major hard date. Exchange deposits, trading access and vesting coins all depend on it.

The pattern

The May 7 casino deadline follows the same structure as every previous deadline in this project. A date is announced. Marketing runs for weeks using that date. The day arrives. No announcement is made that it has happened. Questions in the community go unanswered. A new date appears. This cycle has now repeated across exchange listings, staking, miner delivery, and the casino.

The price on May 7 was approximately $0.00012 — 99.8% below the promised $0.05 launch price. Market cap: $4.9 million. Against a minimum of $100 million raised on-chain.
